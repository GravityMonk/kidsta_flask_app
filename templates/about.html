About Kidsta

Kidsta is a small, safe and fun app made for kids to share pictures and short videos with friends.

What you can do

  • Create your profile with a picture and a Kidsta ID.
  • Search for friends and send friend requests.
  • Upload photos and short videos with a caption.
  • See posts only from people who are your friends.

Safety & privacy

Kidsta keeps things simple and safe. We do not share your location. Your posts are only visible to friends.
